Electricity Consumption Analysis

About Us

Electricity consumption analysis in data analytics is a crucial project that aims to study and understand the patterns of electricity usage over time. By leveraging large-scale data sets, this project seeks to identify trends, anomalies, and factors influencing electricity consumption. The analysis involves data preprocessing, cleaning, and feature engineering to extract meaningful insights. Time series analysis techniques are applied to detect seasonality and periodic trends.The project employs various data visualization tools and statistical methods to interpret consumption patterns, peak hours, and demand variations. Machine learning models, such as regression and clustering, may be utilized to predict future electricity usage and optimize energy management. Additionally, anomaly detection algorithms help in identifying abnormal energy consumption that could indicate equipment malfunctions or inefficiencies.The findings of this project can have significant implications for energy providers, policymakers, and consumers. Improved understanding of electricity usage patterns can lead to better demand forecasting, more efficient resource allocation, and the promotion of sustainable energy practices. Overall, this data analytics project contributes to creating a smarter and more sustainable energy ecosystem.
